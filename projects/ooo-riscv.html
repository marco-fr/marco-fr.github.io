Overview

This is an out-of-order RV32IM processor written in SystemVerilog, built for ECE 411 at UIUC. It implements the explicit register renaming (ERR) microarchitecture where instructions are fetched and decoded in order, renamed onto a larger physical register file, dispatched to reservation stations where they wait for their operands, executed out of order across multiple functional units, and finally retired in order through a reorder buffer.
