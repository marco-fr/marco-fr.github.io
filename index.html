About

I am an incoming M.S. student at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, advised by Prof. Charith Mendis. My research interests are broadly in computer architecture and compilers.

Most of my work has been on the ACT (Accelerator Compiler Toolkit) Ecosystem, which automatically generates software tools from ISA specifications. I co-presented the tutorial on ACT at MICRO 2025 and ASPLOS 2026.

Beyond research, I enjoy working on hardware and low-level systems projects.
