About

I am a incoming M.S student at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, advised by Prof. Charith Mendis. My research interests are broadly in computer architecture and compilers. I have been mainly working on the ACT (Accelerator Compiler Toolkit) Ecosystem, which automatically generates software tools from ISA specifications. I also co-presented the tutorial on ACT at MICRO 2025 and ASPLOS 2026.
